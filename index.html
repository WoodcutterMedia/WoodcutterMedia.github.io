About Us

Understanding what audience like and dislike is probably the most important thing (or) is all what it takes to make a successful movie. This golden decade of the Information age has made it possible to gather tons of data on audience preferences, movies and their performance. At Woodcutter, we bring in the most recent … of the technology world into the movie business (world). The arrival of big data, machine learning and analytics has opened up avenues in a multitude of arenas including the film industry While our labs are continuously researching on innovative products addressing problems in different phases of a film’s lifecycle, currently we offer an end-to-end entertainment analytics platform that simplifies and makes the promotion process a more informed one.

Features

Mentions Tracking and Analysis

Analytics dashboard with real-time updates of mentions on the movie across the public web and comparison with similar movies Powered with NLP and sentiment analysis modules, to feel the pulse of the market.

Control Center

All social media account - Facebook, Youtube, Twitter - in one place. Based on historical data, target audience online behaviour and the live social/political conditions, best time for the campaign is suggested

Script Prediction

Script prediction: Consult on success probability of new scripts, with a statistical accuracy, using machine learning that identifies success patterns based on historical data of successful film scripts
